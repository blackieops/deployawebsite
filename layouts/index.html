So you want to deploy a web site…

The Internet has changed from what it once was. Previously, to get your Web Site On-Line, you just needed to FTP it to a computer with a fancy internet connection, or more recently pay a few dollars a month to a really sketchy company and FTP files around.

While you could still do both of those things, a lot has changed, and you may find yourself dealing with bandwidth and load issues, or simply overwhelmed by the amount of work involved. You may be tempted to use a PaaS or magic SaaS product that promises infinite gain for no work. "No servers required!"

The goal of the guides on this site is to instruct and educate those who don't have a background in systems administration on easy and powerful ways to host a web site on the Internet using modern security and scalability practices. It may not be as hard as you think, and in many cases you might not need servers at all.
